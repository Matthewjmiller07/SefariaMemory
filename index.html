Hebrew Text Memory Game

This game will help you memorize Hebrew texts from the Sefaria database. Enter a text reference, click 'Start Game', and fill in the blanks to test your memory!

Instructions

1. Enter a text reference (e.g., Genesis 1, Genesis 1:1, or Genesis 1:1-2) in the text input field.

2. Click 'Start Game' to begin. The game will display the text with some words hidden.

3. Fill in the blanks with the missing words.

4. Click 'Check Answers' to see your score and compare your answers with the correct answers.
